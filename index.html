Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro GOIH ComM (Portuguese pronunciation: [kɾiʃˈtjɐnu ʁɔˈnaɫdu];
born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier
League club Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team.
Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of
the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or
awards[note 3] and four European Golden Shoes, the moLeaguest by a European player.
